About Foodpi

Foodpi is a complete multi-restaurant food delivery platform designed to streamline online food ordering and restaurant management. It features a Progressive Web App (PWA) website, a powerful Laravel admin panel, and an integrated POS system. With Foodpi, you can efficiently manage orders, menus, payments, deliveries, and customer interactions—all from a single platform.

Progressive Web Application (PWA)

Use your website as a mobile and desktop app—no need to build multiple mobile apps for your business. One website, all solution. Foodpi is built using Progressive Web Application (PWA) and Single-Page Application (SPA) technologies, providing a fast and seamless user experience.

Powerful Point of Sale (POS) System

Manage all your restaurant orders with ease using Foodpi’s advanced POS system. Handle POS and online orders from a single platform without confusion. Track sales, payments, and inventory in real-time. Generate detailed reports and analytics to make informed business decisions. Streamline your operations and serve customers faster. One system, all solution.

Easy Install and Launch:

Get your multi-restaurant food delivery system up and running quickly with a hassle-free installation. Our user-friendly setup wizard and clear documentation make launching your platform simple. Focus on managing restaurants, orders, and deliveries instead of dealing with complex setup steps. Start your food delivery business in minutes, not hours.
